Privacy Policy – The Elite
Last updated: September 21, 2025

The Elite respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store or process any personally identifiable information on our servers.
All game progress and data are stored locally on your device.

2. Advertising

This app displays ads using Google AdMob.
AdMob may collect certain device information (such as advertising identifiers) to deliver and improve ads.
AdMob processes this data according to Google’s own Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device’s ad settings.

3. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
If you are under 13, please do not use this app without parental permission.

4. Data Security

All game data stays on your device. We do not transmit or store personal information on our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will appear on this page and in the app’s Privacy Policy section.
